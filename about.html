Wellcome Sanger Institute

Researching Neglected Tropical Diseases

We are scientists at the Wellcome Sanger Institute, based at Hinxton, near Cambridge, in the UK. We are researching the biology of a number of the organisms that cause Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs).

We hope that the findings from our research will further understanding of these organisms so that ultimately vaccines, drugs and intervention strategies can be used to reduce, or even eliminate, the diseases.

These organisms are also fascinating in their own right, often possessing unique and interesting biology, which we hope to learn more about.
